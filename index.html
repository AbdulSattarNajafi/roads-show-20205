Digital Plus | IT Minus: The Future Made Simple

We come to the Sangfor Roadshow 2025! This year's theme, "Digital Plus | 1T Minus: The Future Made Simple," explores how digitalization can add value to your business while simplifying IT systems and processes.

Digital Plus:

By introducing advanced digital technologies and tools, businesses can enhance their operational efficiency, innovation capabilities, and market competitiveness. At the same time, security solutions play a crucial role in safeguarding digital assets, ensuring data protection, and maintaining compliance. Digital transformation will bring more opportunities and growth points, enabling companies to respond more quickly to market changes and customer needs.

IT Minus:

In the journey towards digitalization, it's crucial to simplity and optimize existing IT and security systems and processes. Join us as we discuss strategies to reduce unnecessary complexity and costs. By streamlining IT architecture and processes, businesses can focus more on their core operations, improving overall efficiency and flexibility.

Join us at the Sangfor Roadshow 2025 to experience the transformative power of digitalization combined with simplified IT solutions.

Don't miss this opportunity to see how "Digital Plus | IT Minus" can make the future of your organization simpler and secure. Join us at the Sangfor Roadshow 2025 and take the first step towards a smarter, more streamlined digital transformation.

Guy​ Rosefelt

Sangfor Chief Product Officer

Tian Koon Lee
